About Noble Slate

Now

Hello there! My name is George-Owen Damoyi-Bouridis. This page is my logbook and my corner of this vast internet. It is part portfolio, part notebook, and part memory for future me. If I did something, it will be shown here. If I am still learning, it should be mention say that.

This site is called Noble Slate, originig from my name "George-Owen" as you should know by now. “George” comes from an old Greek root for “earth worker”[1]. “Owen” is tied to names that mean “well-born” or “noble”[2]. A slate[3] is a rock that holds a straight line under pressure, or a page you write on, erase, and write again. Put it together and you get how I try to work on everything I come across in life. That is the point of this page.

Right now

I am drafting a simple thrust-vector-controlled model rocket I can recover completely after usage. I am in progress on designing Two-custom servo gimbal sketches, a PID controller outline, an IMU short-list, a first control-loop plan, and a ground test checklist. Failure notes will be linked on this site.

I am currently developing an early wearable health device called VitaTrace. I have developped my clear concept, service flow, and overall product. My father's passing when I was younger from pancreatic cancer has prompted me to pursue such project, which is why I care about revolutionizing early signals and preventing subtle changes becoming a life or death decision, all from something easily missed.

I am getting more confident on tuba as well[10]. There's a log on how to play the tuba found on this site, intended for beginners to follow them.
